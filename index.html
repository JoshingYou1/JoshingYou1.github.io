Insaiyan Physique

This workout app allows the user to utilize various exercises based on the different muscle groups of the body. It provides a description of each exercise, images depicting the proper range of motion, the required equipment, the muscle(s) targeted by the exercise along with a helpful diagram of those muscle(s), additional comments, and instructional videos on how to properly perform the exercise. This app will help the user develop the perfect workout regimen for whatever goals they may have.

Nephrology Nexus

This app aims to provide a more efficient way for nephrologists/nurse practitioners and hemodialysis clinics to communicate by allowing them to access and manipulate data regarding dialysis clinics and dialysis patient information, including those patient's lab results. Registered users have the ability to create, read, update, and delete data within the database. The manipulatable data available in this application includes clinical data, patient data, and lab results data. With all of this power at the user's finger tips, there's no telling how much progress can be made toward bringing doctors and healthcare facilities closer together!

Dialysis and Me

This app was designed to provide end-stage renal disease(ESRD) patients with the ability to take control of their illness and increase their level of self-care. Registered patients have access to their personal, insurance, and treatment info, doctors, appointments, lab results, and a plethora of patient education information. The easier it is for patients to manage their medical needs, the quicker they can get back to living their lives.
