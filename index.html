Hi, I'm Joshua Drumm. I'm a fullstack developer, gamer, and OOP enthusiast.

I recently attended the fullstack-flex bootcamp at Thinkful. I have a passion for making people's lives more efficient through technology and developing software to meet their needs allows me to do just that. The most satifying part of programming is the feeling I get when I finally debug that piece of code that kept me up at night for hours on end. I love learning new languages and frameworks to help bring my ideas to life as well as stay relevant in this fast-paced field. I'm currently learning Ruby on Rails and it has been a blast.

When I'm not coding or thinking of potential app ideas, I can be found immersed in a variety of video games, working out at the local gym, or listening to my favorite tunes. I will never stop learning and improving as a programmer and I can't wait to see what the future holds. Happy coding!
